How did Liberia become to be?

how about we start from the very beginning of how Liberia became to be. Nothing better than a quick introduction, am i right?.

from the very beginning

Liberia was founded in 1822 by the American Colonization Society. This society group was solely created to support the relocation of freed slaves and rescued Africans and Caribbeans from slave ships back to Africa. For this reasoning, Liberia was used as a settlement country. The freed slaves were referred to as “Americo-Liberians” while the rescued Africans and Caribbeans became known as “Congo’s.” in 1824, the settlement had been renamed as Monrovia, which is now the capital, and the colony became named as Liberia, which means “the land of the free”.

what happened after Americo-Liberians came?

When Americo-Liberians came to Liberia they began to exclude the Indigenous people there. The Indigenous people were considered uncivilized and were stopped from participating in political and social life such as not having the right to vote. They were also treated based on the color of their skin. This structure had many characteristics of a white supremacy ideology that was done in the west.
